# AI as an Orchestration Layer

A key design principle of the OAN AI module is that AI does not act as a source of truth. Instead, it determines which systems or data sources to invoke and how to present the resulting information to the user.

All responses generated by the AI are:

* Grounded in knowledge base sourced from trusted institutions (for advisory and schemes)
* Backed by live API calls (for weather, prices, status checks, and grievances)
* Constrained by domain-specific guardrails
* Safe and responsible&#x20;

This ensures that:

* Information remains verifiable and auditable
* The system avoids hallucinated or speculative outputs
* Source attribution can be maintained where required
* System is free of any biases

This design aligns with OAN’s broader philosophy of building trustworthy digital public infrastructure, where intelligence augments systems rather than replacing them.
