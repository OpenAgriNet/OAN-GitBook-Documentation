# Onboarding Steps



On boarding onto the OAN network involves ,&#x20;

**Step 1:**  Participants needs to install , setup and configure the Beckn protocol server (Please refer to [Installation of Seeker](installation-steps-seeker.md)  or [installation of Provider](installation-steps-provider.md) depending on the role you will play) \
\
**Step 2:** Post the installation , please send an email to _**oansocial@coss.org.in**_ with the following information.\
\
**Subject line :** Request for Sandbox Access – \[Organisation Name]\
\
🏢 Organization Name: \[Enter Organization Name] \
\
👤 Contact Person’s Name: \[Enter Name]\
\
&#x20;📧 Email: \[Enter Email]\
\
&#x20;📞 Phone Number: \[Enter Phone Number] \
\
💡 Reason for Participating on the Network: \[Briefly Describe Your Purpose] \
\
📍 Organization Address: \[Enter Address] \
\
🔹 Network Participant Type: 🕵️‍♂️ Seeker /  📤 Provider –  \[ Enter Seeker or Provider]\
\
🌐 Fully Qualified Domain Name (FQDN) of Your Instance:  \[Enter FQDN]



**Step 3:**  **Verification & Account Activation** \
📌 The OAN team will review and verify the submitted details. ✅ Upon approval, your account will be activated in the Staging/Development environment, and you will be notified.<br>

**Step 4:** You can now do the testing/creating APIs(if you are a provider) etc.. and make sure that your setup is working and you are able to do the transactions.\
\
