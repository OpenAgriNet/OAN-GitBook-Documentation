# Roles You Can Play

An entity can play various roles in VISTAAR. The primary roles are:

**Provider Platform:** If you create or facilitate agriculture-related verified content, best practices, video scheme information, farming techniques, etc, and aspire to broaden the reach to farmers, governments, and field-level workers via a web page, mobile or web application or would want to build one in future, you can join VISTAAR as a Provider Platform.

**Provider:** If you'd like to be onboarded on an existing provider platform as one of the providers of the agri-related contents you can join the network as a Provider.

**Seeker Platform:** If you are an application or a platform provider that acts as an interface for farmers, students, and field-level workers to access information, and advisory resources for agriculture from various sources or aspire to become a platform that can enable access to agriculture resources, content, advisory services available on the network, you can join VISTAAR as a seeker platform.

**Seeker:** If you'd like to consume the information made available by the VISTAAR network through any of the seeker platforms, you can join as a seeker.

**Network Service Provider:** If you choose to enable other organizations to join this network through your technology, business or onboarding services, this role will suit you the best.

You have the flexibility to choose one of the aforementioned roles or even assume more than one role within the network, enabling you to actively engage in and benefit from the diverse opportunities provided by the network.
